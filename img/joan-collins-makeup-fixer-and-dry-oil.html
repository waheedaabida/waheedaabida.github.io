JOAN COLLINS MAKEUP FIXER AND DRY OIL BODY POLISH

Sunday, 6 November 2016

Joan Collins is an 83 year old English actress, those of you who grew up in my generation may know her from Footballers Wives or currently from the famous Snickers advertise on TV. Her skincare, makeup and fragrance range was created to suit every woman, no matter what age. 

 TEN EFFECTS SKIN TREAT AND MAKEUP FIXER £18  |  purchase here
"TEN EFFECTS Skin Treat and Makeup Fixer promises ten skin benefits whilst locking make-up in place in just one spritz. Light, luscious and luxurious, ten effects is the perfect partner for those wanting beautifully hydrated skin with flawless make-up all day long".
If you've been following my blog and social media for a while, you'll know how much I love facial mists. When I was sent this a a couple of months back, I was really intrigued by the ten different skin benefits it claims to have; super hydrating, firms, refines wrinkles, minimises pores, improves tone, adds PH balancing layer, softens, calms redness, locks makeup and cools the skin down.

 Since using the mist, I can definitely say it locks the makeup in place, hydrates the skin and refreshes the makeup look (a little like the infamous Mac Fix+ but with extra benefits). The spray consists of three of the most popular skin loving ingredients; rose water, Hyaluronic acid and glycerin. I apply my foundation as normal, spritz this directly onto my face and the dab my beautyblender to work the product into my skin. After long days at work and meeting friends after, I spray this onto my face to refresh and somewhat 'redo' my makeup. 

 I AM WOMAN DRY OIL BODY POLISH  |  £16  |  purchase here
"With Himalayan Salts, Kiwi Seeds and Avocado, Olive and Jojoba Oils, indulge in a beautiful, at home, spa treatment that imparts a youthful brightness and leaves skin feeling silky smooth and softly fragrant". 
There's nothing I love more than a good body scrub thats really rough - especially ones that are infused with oils so I don't need to moisturise afterwards. A little goes a long way with this particular scrub. It leaves the skin feeling super, super soft and as you scrub your body the salts release a gorgeous smell which lingers on the skin for a good 2-3 hours after washing it off. The scrub definitely has a luxury feel to it, maybe its the heavy glass packaging it's in, and it does a great job of removing all the dead skin, leaving the body feeling smooth.

 Have you tried any of the Joan Collin's skincare products?
